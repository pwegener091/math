About me

I am a mathematician who obtained his PhD in 2017 at Bielefeld University under the supervision of Barbara Baumeister.

From 2017 to 2020 I was a Postdoc in the working group of Gunter Malle at TU Kaiserslautern. From 2020 to 2025 I was a Postdoc in the Institute of Algebra, Number Theory and Discrete Mathematics at Leibniz University Hannover

My main research interest are Coxeter groups and problems related to these groups.

Research interests

  • Coxeter groups
  • Elliptic Weyl groups
  • (Algebraic) Combinatorics
